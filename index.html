I'm a PhD student at IRIT in Toulouse, France, where I work on computer vision, specifically image processing and 3D reconstruction. My research focuses on space navigation problems. I'm funded by an ESA OSIP grant and supervised by Géraldine Morin and Simone Gasparini.
